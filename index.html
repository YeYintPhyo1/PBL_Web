Why Should We Eat Healthy Food 🔥

How to Build a Balanced Diet for a Stronger Body and Mind

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates give your body the energy it needs to stay active and focused.

Proteins

Proteins help build muscles and repair cells, keeping your body strong.

Fats

Fats provide energy, support brain health, and help absorb vitamins.

Vitamins

Vitamins protect your body by boosting immunity and keeping organs healthy.

Minerals

Minerals strengthen bones, teeth, and support nerve and muscle function.

Water

Water keeps you hydrated, helps digestion, and controls body temperature.
